Contact Me

Hello!

Thanks for visiting my builder portfolio! My name is Bo and I live in Hyattsville, Maryland with my wife and three cats. I am transmasculine and use they/them pronouns.

During the day I am a web developer at a DC based media company, and during the weekends and evenings I am a hobby woodworker and builder.

I love using tools, creating and problem solving. This portfolio is a place for me to show off some of my projects and hopefully reach people who I can work with to keep on building.

Woodwork

I have experience building small to medium sized furniture (consoles, benches, side tables, vanities, etc) as well as sculptures like bandsaw boxes, toys, and hand tools such as joinery mallets.

Construction

I have experience doing basic plumbing fixes and electrical installations, as well as drywall repair, painting, hanging doors, casing & trim, and installing engineering hardwood floors.

Web Development

I have 10+ years of experience in web development, particularly Django/Python, Bootsrap, JQuery, and AngularJS. While I prefer to spend my freetime building physical things, I am also open to building web software.
